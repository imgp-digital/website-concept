Investment Philosophy

Asset Preservation Advisors (APA) believe that persistent pricing inefficiencies exist in the municipal bond market, especially in the secondary market where the firm can be nimbler. They seek to capitalize on these inefficiencies to identify investment opportunities and create value through rigorous credit research, active portfolio management and relative-value trading discipline.
