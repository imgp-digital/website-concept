Investment Philosophy

Berkshire believe there is an epidemic for managers and funds in the dividend and dividend growth space, and that countless processes begin with over simplified screens. Their view is that simple screens of historical data may fail to capture the full dividend growth opportunity set, so they attempt to identify perennial dividend growers before they are fully recognized by the market using a forward looking, bottom-up selection process.

Strategies

Berkshire Dividend Growth Strategy

Seeks to generate a growing stream of income through investments in a diversified portfolio of equities with a stable, high and growing dividend.
