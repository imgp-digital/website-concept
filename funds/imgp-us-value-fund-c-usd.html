Underlying strategy

Scharf Sustainable Value Equity

The iMGP US Value fund seeks to identify companies with low valuations combined with growing earnings, cash flow and/or book value. The investment team seeks to provide capital appreciation over the course of an entire market cycle while losing notably less than relevant benchmarks in falling markets.
